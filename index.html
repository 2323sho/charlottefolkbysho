About Us

Charlotte Folk is a dynamic local streetwear brand rooted in the vibrant culture of the Philippines. Our mission is to create high-quality, stylish apparel that reflects the unique spirit and identity of Filipino street culture. Inspired by the streets, art, and the diverse communities of the Philippines, we blend traditional and contemporary influences to craft clothing that resonates with authenticity and pride. Our designs are a celebration of Filipino heritage, incorporating local motifs, colors, and stories into modern streetwear fashion. At Charlotte Folk, we believe in supporting local artisans and using sustainable practices to ensure that every piece of clothing not only looks good but also does good for the community and the environment. Join us in wearing your culture proudly and making a statement with every piece. Charlotte Folk is more than just a brand; it's a movement that champions the creativity, resilience, and spirit of the Filipino people.


Projects

Charlotte Folk Philippines is dedicated to a range of projects that merge creativity, community, and sustainability. We collaborate with local artists and designers to produce limited-edition streetwear collections that celebrate Filipino culture, while our pop-up shops and events engage the community directly. Our Sustainable Fashion Initiative focuses on eco-friendly production and a recycling program for old apparel, promoting environmental responsibility. Additionally, our Cultural Heritage Series designs collections inspired by various Filipino regions and traditions, complemented by workshops and talks to deepen community understanding of our rich cultural heritage.
