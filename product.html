Projects

Charlotte Folk Philippines is dedicated to a range of projects that merge creativity, community, and sustainability. We collaborate with local artists and designers to produce limited-edition streetwear collections that celebrate Filipino culture, while our pop-up shops and events engage the community directly. Our Sustainable Fashion Initiative focuses on eco-friendly production and a recycling program for old apparel, promoting environmental responsibility. Additionally, our Cultural Heritage Series designs collections inspired by various Filipino regions and traditions, complemented by workshops and talks to deepen community understanding of our rich cultural heritage.
